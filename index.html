How to Play

  1. Place your Ante bet (Blind = Ante automatically). Optional Trips+ side bet.
  2. Click Deal Cards. You see your 2 hole cards.
  3. Pre-flop: Bet 4x or 3x your Ante as the Play bet, or Check.
  4. Flop (3 cards): If you haven't bet, Bet 2x or Check.
  5. Turn & River (2 more cards): If you haven't bet, Bet 1x or Fold.
  6. Showdown: Dealer reveals cards. Dealer needs a pair to qualify.

Payouts (if you win)

  • Ante: 1:1 (push if dealer doesn't qualify)
  • Blind: See pay table (push for pairs/trips/straights when undealt community)
  • Play: Always 1:1
