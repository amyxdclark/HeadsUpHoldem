How to Play

Overview

  1. Place an Ante bet and an optional Trips+ side bet, then click Deal.
  2. A Blind bet equal to your Ante is placed automatically.
  3. Pre-Flop: Bet 4x or 3x on strong hands, or Check (pass).
  4. Flop: Bet 2x or Check after seeing 3 community cards.
  5. River: Bet 1x or Fold after seeing all 5 community cards.
  6. Best 5-card poker hand wins the showdown!

Dealer Qualification

  • Dealer must have a pair or better to qualify.
  • If the dealer doesn't qualify, the Ante pushes and Play bet wins 1:1.

Blind Bonus Pays

  • Straight: 1:1  |  Flush: 3:2  |  Full House: 3:1
  • Four of a Kind: 10:1  |  Straight Flush: 50:1  |  Royal Flush: 500:1
